Let's talk a little about what symptoms a slow computer can have.

Over 92% of computers are infected with Adware and spyware. Such software
is rarely accompanied by uninstall utility and even when it almost always
leaves broken Windows Registry keys behind it. Even if you have an
anti-spyware tool, your Windows Registry might be broken.
